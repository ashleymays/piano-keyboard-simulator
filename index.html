What is Online Piano?

Online Piano is a virtual piano that works on desktop and mobile browsers. The goal of this app is to let you play a piano with intuitive controls and high quality sounds. This app was inspired by OnlinePianist.com, Recursive Artsâ€™ virtual piano, and VirtualPiano.net among others.

How to Play

You can use your desktop keyboard to play, or you can use your mouse or fingers to press the notes on the virtual keyboard. There are several options you can choose from:

  • soften: When selected the audio starts at a lower volume.
  • sustain: The audio plays for an extended amount of time even when the key is released.
  • keyboard: Displays the keyboard inputs that correspond to each key on the virtual keyboard.
  • record: Record and download your playing to your computer.
  • octave controls: The controls displayed below the keyboard. The left and right arrows shift the keys up one octave and down one octave, respectively.

You can also choose one of four instrument sounds: Acoustic Grand Piano, Electric Piano, Music Box, and Harp.
